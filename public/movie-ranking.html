The Best Movie According to Gizachew

My Top 3 of all Time.

Friends

This is my favorite TV show of all time. It is a comedy show that aired from 1994 to 2004. It has 10 seasons and 236 episodes. It is about a group of friends living in New York City. The main characters are Ross, Rachel, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe. The show is very funny and has a lot of memorable moments. I have watched it multiple times and never get tired of it.

Avengers

This is my favorite movie of all time. It is a superhero movie that was released in 2012. It is about a group of superheroes who come together to save the world from an alien invasion.

Frozen

This is my favorite animated movie of all time. It is a Disney movie that was released in 2013. It is about a princess named Anna who goes on a journey to find her sister Elsa, who has magical ice powers. The movie has great songs and a heartwarming story.
